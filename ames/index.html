If you are making a film for your Nat 5 media course or your higher media work then that film is eligible for the SYFF AMES awards. Make sure it is under 7 mins (no copyrighted music please) and when you have it ready then ask your teacher to submit it at the appropriate time.

You'll have to get it put in for marking first but once that's been done it can be submitted to SYFF via Film Freeway.

Who knows? You can maybe join us at the festival to see your film on a big screen and in with a chance of picking up a prestigious (and oh so expensive) SYFF AMES award like Miriam here.
