Change

COP26 is coming to Glasgow in November and SYFF have partnered with the National Library of Scotland’s Moving Image Archive and ScottishPower to launch a film competition designed to give young Scots a voice at this historic event.

We want you to make a film on the theme of Change. How does climate change impact you? Have you changed your behaviour because of it? What do we need to change to ensure a greener future?

It’s up to you.



Shortlisted films will be announced at this year’s Scottish Youth Film Festival and winning films will receive their prizes at a special event to be held during COP26.

Please don’t use copyrighted music.

You can access free, non-copyright music by clicking on the following links, from sites like bensound.com and Royalty Free Music | No Copyright Music on Soundcloud and YouTube.

For free sound effects go to freesound.org

Please credit the artists and the website in your credits.

You can also source free stills and clips from pexels.com and vimeo.com/groups/freehd

Click on the FilmFreeway link below to upload your films. Please click on the option to make them downloadable.

Good Luck
