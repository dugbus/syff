Change

The Change competition films were announced at the Scottish Youth Film Festival on October 23rd and 24th.

Films will be screened at a Gala event in the Green Zone at COP26 on the 7th November. Two films will be chosen as overall winners and all films will be awarded prizes courtesy of our supporters.
