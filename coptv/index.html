COP TV

In 2021, SYFF trained and deployed 11 young people at the COP 26 climate summit in Glasgow. They produced a series of short films in the lead up to and during COP and every day from Monday 8th to Friday 12th November they live-streamed an hour long magazine style show with studio guests and curated content.

You can see their programmes and some of the short films here - https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCAdqoEVozVbmVaqEb3ZsGkg/videos
