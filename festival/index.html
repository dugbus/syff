The Festival

The 2021 Scottish Youth Film Festival is now open for entries. To submit your films please click on the FilmFreeway link below. The Deadline is 31st August 2021 and films will be judged by a jury of young people from Into Film’s Youth Advisory Council. You must be resident in Scotland and aged 19 or under to enter. Films must be 7 minutes or shorter.

Awards are given in the categories of Live Action, Animation and Documentary across age ranges 12 and under and 13-19. A Best Film Award is also handed out for each age category.

This year, we are also accepting films made for Nat 5 and Higher Media. These films can be any length.

Please don’t use copyrighted music.

You can access free, non-copyright music by clicking on the following links, from sites like bensound.com and Royalty Free Music | No Copyright Music on Soundcloud and YouTube.

For free sound effects for to freesound.org

Please credit the artists and the website in your credits.

You can also source free stills and clips from pexels.com and vimeo.com/groups/freehd

Click on the FilmFreeway link below to upload your films. make sure and select the right category for your film and make them downloadable.

The Scottish Youth Film Festival is a celebration of the work of young Scots and centres around a weekend of activity where young people can come along and try their hand at filmmaking, speak to people in the industry, network with their peers and find out how they can take their interests further.
