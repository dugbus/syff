The Festival

The 8th Scottish Youth Film Festival will take place in Dumfries from 21st -23rd October 2022.

We open on Friday night with a special screening of Tremors at the Robert Burns Centre at 6pm.

On Saturday 22nd, it’s the day for 13-19 year olds and we’ll have workshops, panels, screenings and the Award ceremony. We’ll be joined by John McPhail (director of Anna and the Apocalypse), Raisha Ahmed (writer & director) as well as many other special guests.

On Sunday, we’ll be focusing on the 12 and under age range with lots of hands on film making activities, screenings and the Awards.

It’s all free and we’d love to see you there.
