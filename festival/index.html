The Festival

The 8th annual Scottish Youth Film Festival took place at The Bridge in Dumfries on the 22nd and 23rd October.

We had an incredible line up of guests, workshops and practical film making and the awesome DGTV team did two live shows which you can watch on our YouTube channel.

More pictures, reports and films coming soon but in the meantime, here are the awards recipients for this year.

Well done to all of the filmmakers who entered and we look forward to seeing all of your films next year.
