The Festival

Our 9th annual Scottish Youth Film Festival will take place in Perth on the 29th and 30th September 2023.

Film industry guests, workshops, screenings and other events will be announced in the run up.

If you live in Scotland, are aged 19 or under and have a film of 7 minutes or under, you can submit it via the link below.

There's still time to make a film as the deadline is August 18th.





Please don't use copyrighted music or images. There are free resources all over the internet but to get you started.
