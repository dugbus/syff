Films For Our Future

The Scottish Youth Film Foundation in partnership with WWF Scotland and the National Library of Scotland’s Moving Image Archive and in collaboration with The Redford Center want to see your films about our future.

Climate change is happening but what will happen next?

We want to know what you think. A technological utopia? A green paradise? Or are we heading for disaster?

Make a film of five minutes or under and submit it to us via the link below.

Winning films will be preserved as part of the National Library of Scotland's Moving Image Archive, will be shown at the Scottish Youth Film Festival and will take home a selection of prizes.
