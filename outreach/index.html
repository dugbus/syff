Outreach and Education

Our Outreach projects target young people who would not normally have the opportunity and resources to make a film. We work with them on analysing films and learning how films work, before developing a story and teaching them the roles required to produce a film. With a script written, the young people cast, act in and film themselves to create the end product and we always end in a celebration.

We also teach film in school settings, helping teacher and students alike unlock the literacy of films and teaching them to be able to ‘read’ and ‘write’ films using the resources that their classrooms have. Tablets and phones allow anyone to make a film and easy to use apps can unlock a world of creativity.
